Welcome to CalCentral

You are a Delegate

CalCentral is the portal for the University of California, Berkeley. If you are seeing this view of CalCentral, you have been designated as a Delegate by a Berkeley student. Delegates are often parents, guardians or other trusted adults.

You may be a Delegate for more than one student.

Using CalCentral

Your Privileges

A student (your Delegator) has delegated privileges to you. Your privileges may include features such as paying bills and viewing the student's schedule.

Viewing as a Student

To view information for a student ("Act As"), click on the student's name in the Students card. The information shown in CalCentral will then reflect the student. You will see new menu items such as My Finances at the top of the page. There will be an overlay at the bottom of each page that indicates which student you are Acting As. To stop Acting As, in the overlay click Stop.

User Menu

The user menu is at the top right of this page. It has your name as its title.

Editing your Contact Information

To view and edit information about you such as your mailing address, choose Profile from the user menu.

Signing Out

To sign out of CalCentral, choose Log Out from the user menu.
